Responsive App Layout

Content railUse --content-width: min(100% - clamp(2rem, 5vw, 5rem), 112rem)
HeaderBrand and nav live in .topbar and align to the same rail as main content
DesktopCenter pages, widen panels, increase card minimums, and scale type/spacing at >=80rem
MobileStack .topbar and nav below 42rem; keep forms/cards touch-friendly and full-width
ButtonsAll buttons use the shared hover/active treatment; row-specific actions such as bottle refill belong inline with their row
